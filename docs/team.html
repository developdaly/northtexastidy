Meet North Texas Tidy

North Texas Tidy is a locally owned and operated home cleaning business based in Denton, TX. Our mission is to make life easier for our neighbors by providing reliable, high-quality cleaning services with a personal touch. We believe a clean home is a happy home, and we’re passionate about helping our community shine.

Who We Are

Founded by Denton locals, North Texas Tidy was created to bring a new level of professionalism and care to home cleaning. Our team is made up of friendly, trustworthy professionals who are background-checked, insured, and trained to deliver a sparkling clean every time.

  • Locally owned and operated
  • Fully insured and background-checked cleaners
  • Flexible scheduling and transparent pricing
  • Committed to customer satisfaction

Our Mission

To provide Denton and the surrounding North Texas area with dependable, high-quality cleaning services that make life easier and homes healthier. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients through trust, transparency, and exceptional service.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We do what we say, and we treat your home with the utmost respect.
  • Reliability: You can count on us to show up on time and deliver consistent results.
  • Community: We’re proud to serve Denton and support our local community.
  • Quality: We never cut corners—your satisfaction is our top priority.

Meet Patrick Daly

Patrick Daly founded North Texas Tidy with a vision to make home cleaning simple, trustworthy, and accessible for everyone in Denton. With a background in customer service and a passion for helping others, Patrick is dedicated to building a business that puts people first—both clients and cleaners. When he’s not working, you’ll find him exploring Denton’s local spots or volunteering in the community.
